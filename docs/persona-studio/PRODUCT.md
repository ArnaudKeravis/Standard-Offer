# Persona Studio — Product

> Create, understand and design with the people you serve.

Persona Studio is a premium research + design tool used by Sodexo CoDesign
teams to prepare and facilitate client workshops. It is **not** a generic AI
dashboard: it is meant to be shown in executive presentations and used live in
facilitated sessions.

## What it does

1. **Create** evidence-based personas (guided, AI-assisted, or by hand).
2. **Visualize** and compare personas (gallery, editorial detail, compare,
   needs map, journey lens, presentation mode).
3. **Talk to** a persona through a research-grounded AI simulation.
4. **Use** personas live in workshops to assess ideas, journeys and concepts.

## Two persona families, one product

| Family | Example | Theme |
|---|---|---|
| Corporate / Workplace | The Leader, The Conductor, … (Personix archetypes) | Sodexo blue, calm & structured |
| Sports Hospitality (Tour de France) | David Richardson, Sophie Lambert, Thomas Garcia, Claire Dubois | White/black + per-guest accent |

Both families share a **stable persona core** and differ only through
configurable **templates**, **sections** and **themes** — never through forked
code.

## Core principles (non-negotiable)

1. **Behaviour first** — personas are differentiated by behaviour, needs and
   tensions, not by age/gender/title.
2. **Evidence transparency** — every statement is `EVIDENCE`, `ASSUMPTION` or
   `TO_VALIDATE`, and you can see where it came from.
3. **No fake research** — generated assumptions are never presented as findings.
4. **Quote integrity** — quotes are typed: verbatim / composite / drafted
   hypothesis / none.
5. **Confidence with explanation** — HIGH / MEDIUM / LOW, always explained.
6. **Workshop utility** — every screen helps a team decide.
7. **Human editability** — anything AI generates stays editable.
8. **Traceability** — sources and evidence are always inspectable.
9. **Anti-stereotype control** — demographic shortcuts are flagged.
10. **AI as simulation, not truth** — "Talk to the persona" is a grounded
    simulation, never a real customer or employee.
